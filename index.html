--- layout: default title: AI-BOM Inspector --- # Meet the AI-BOM Inspector AI-BOM Inspector is your command center for understanding the software that powers your supply chain. In a single view, you can surface vulnerabilities, license risks, and component health across every bill of materials (BOM) you manage. ## Why AI-BOM Inspector? - **Instant visibility:** Upload a SBOM and get prioritized findings in seconds. - **AI-assisted analysis:** Natural-language summaries translate complex dependency data into clear actions. - **Governance ready:** Align with industry standards while keeping audit trails organized. - **Secure by design:** Built with a security-first mindset so sensitive dependency data stays protected. ## What you can do right now 1. **Inspect a BOM:** Drop in your SBOM file to get a fast, AI-driven risk report. 2. **Trace dependencies:** Understand how every component fits together, including transitive relationships. 3. **Plan remediation:** Export recommended fixes and ownership details for engineering and vendor teams. ## How it works 1. **Ingest** — AI-BOM Inspector parses CycloneDX and SPDX SBOMs, normalizing component data for consistent review. 2. **Analyze** — Vulnerabilities, licenses, and metadata are enriched with AI summaries to highlight what matters. 3. **Decide** — Dashboard views and downloadable reports keep stakeholders aligned on risk and remediation priorities. ## Built for your workflow - Works alongside existing CI/CD and supply-chain security tools. - Clear documentation and examples for integrating automated SBOM checks. - Human-friendly reports you can share with leadership, partners, and auditors. ## Get updates Want to know when new capabilities drop?
